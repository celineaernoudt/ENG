Full carpet framework

videoperformance / videoinstallation /  1h20m26s

(2016)


During this videoperformance, the surface is transformed in 7 fases. First, thereâ€™s a green car- pet rolled up, second the surface is being sprinkled, third the sand is shoveled, fourth the sand is smoothened, fifth the surface is wrapped in plastic, sixth the surface is whitened and seventh a green carpet is unrolled.


This work has been created as an extension of the work Framing of an inartificial green key, but deviates from it because of its transformation as a measure, besides the measuring as a measure. This videoperformance is made on another inclined surface, where several actions took place. These actions point out the malleability, beside the more subtle suggested clarification of perspective and depth. Malleability in a immediate way, as well as on frame.
