Difficult but all yours

puzzles and happening

(2017)


Limited edition of 5 puzzles of me crying, put in handmade boxes, raffled at my exhibition "Goedenavond Enchant√© Auf Wiedersehen". One ticket cost one euro. The 117 (also the amount of puzzle pieces) losing tickets showed a tear, the 5 winning tickets showed a puzzle piece. All puzzles were taken on the 24th of February at Stellwerk, Kassel.


This work is not much more than a (word) game. The word "difficult" refers to the level of of the puzzle, the raffle and how I feel, as all yours refers to the puzzle pieces and me being at your command, after winning.
